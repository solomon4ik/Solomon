Ternopil Ukraine

Ternopil is a city located in the center of Western Ukraine, an administrative, economic and cultural center of the Ternopil region, one of the three main centers of the historic region of Galicia. The population of Ternopil is about 217,000 (2016), the area - 72 sq. km.
